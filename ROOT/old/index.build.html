How to Develop Property in Metagascar


We are committed to open-source technology for flexibility and upgradability. We believe this technology will improve over time, allowing us to migrate with ease. Currently, there are several tools available to our community that help them realize their virtual dreams.

If a community member wishes to customize a property and build from scratch, they can start with Blender, Autocad and Mozilla Spoke software. Within the Mozilla Spoke environment, one can discover and explore 3D models for Sketchfab and Google Poly. Members are in complete control of their creation.

Alternatively, 3D homes are available for purchase from Sketchfab and Google Poly with a low poly count that requires minimal editing. If editing is required, import the 3D image to Blender for final touches or reduce the poly count, i.e. remove furniture, adjust scene, change color, etc. Upon completion, the 3D image can be uploaded to Sketchfab. Next, ensure that the 3D model matches the on-chain data (square-foot) of the property NFT. Upon completion, in Mozilla Spoke, publish the project to Mozilla Hubs. Then submit the Mozilla Hubs URL link to Metagascar for approval. Lastly, the Metagascar team will update the Web3 Ecosystem linking the NFT with the VR experience to your property.
